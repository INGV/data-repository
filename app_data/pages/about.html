Welcome to the INGV Open Data Repository

At INGV we have set up an Open Data Repository in line with the FAIR principles, ensuring that our research products are:

  • Findable: our user-friendly interface facilitates easy navigation, allowing researchers to swiftly locate and access relevant datasets;
  • Accessible: emphasizing inclusivity, we strive to eliminate barriers to information. Our repository ensures that data is readily available to all members of the research community;
  • Interoperable: by employing standardized formats and metadata, we enhance the interoperability of our datasets, promoting seamless integration into diverse research workflows;
  • Reusable: recognizing the value of sustainability in scientific endeavors, our repository encourages the responsible and ethical reuse of data, contributing to the longevity and impact of research outputs.

This ensures that datasets stored becomes easily discoverable, accessible, and seamlessly integrable across various platforms.
